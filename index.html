About Me

I live in the California Central Valley, where I am surrounded by grassy fields, beautiful hills, mountains and within driving distance to the sunny California beaches. I have worked in labs using fruitflies, blue-green algae, bacteria, sea anemones, sea slugs, and california mussels. I received my B.S. degree from the University of California, Merced and my M.S. degree from the California State University Fresno. For my master thesis I analyzed the locomotion of fruitflies that were given BMAA in their food. The fruitflies were treated with the bioamine octopamine prior to recording their walking paths.
